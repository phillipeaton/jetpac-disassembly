6810: Default explosion SFX parameters.
These parameters are used in pairs: SFX #1 is used for most aliens, while SFX #2 is used for player, sphere alien, and crossed ship.
Bytes(n) Parameter
1 SFX 1: Frequency
2 SFX 1: Duration
3 SFX 2: Frequency
4 SFX 2: Duration
